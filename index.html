ABOUT
THP

The Harmony Place (THP) is a non-profit helping adults dealing with the effects of trauma and PTSD.

THP was started by a woman who noticed the need for trauma and PTSD support in our community. She gathered a group of individuals who share the same passion to help. Together they created a structured support group open to the public.

Learn More

OUR
SERVICES

The Harmony Place offers a free peer-to-peer support group for those in the community who suffer or have suffered from the debilitation effects of trauma.

Soon we will be adding yoga, meditation, and self-defense classes to our services as well to aid in the release of trauma while strengthening both the mind and the body.

Learn More

OUR
BLOG

We post a recurring blog through our Medium account where we address and discuss the typical issues a person that has suffered or is currently suffering from trauma may face.

Feel free to engage us about any of the topics we cover. If you have a specific topic you would like addressed, we would be happy to open the discussion.
