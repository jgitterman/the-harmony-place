ABOUT THP

The Harmony Place (THP) was started by a single woman who noticed the need for trauma and PTSD support in the community. She gathered a group of individuals who share the same passion to help those in need. Together they created a structured support group open to the public.

Learn More

OUR SERVICES

The Harmony Place offers a free peer-to-peer support group for those in the community who suffer or have suffered from the debilitation effects of trauma. We will soon be adding yoga, mediation, and self-defense classes to our services as well.
