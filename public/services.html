Our Services

Support Group

Meets every Tuesday Starting on 4/2
6:30 pm - 7:30 pm
Residing at
The Bayard Rustin Center for Social Justice

The Harmony Place (THP) welcomes any adult who has suffered from the debilitating effects of trauma or PTSD. It is a safe place where each person can share their experiences, struggles, and achievements. There are no registration or fee requirements. Attendees are encouraged to share. However, no one will be forced to speak until they are ready. THP is a peer to peer support group, NOT group therapy. At the end of each session, there will be 20 minutes of meditation and mindfulness. If you have any questions, please contact us.

Please bear in mind that the diagnosis and treatment of PTSD and other psychiatric disorders require trained medical professionals.
